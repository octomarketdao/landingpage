One DEX on Many Chains

OctoMarkets order & price data data are synchronized across supported blockchains by the protocol relayer. You can open a position on one chain, and then close the position on another, paying gas in their native tokens, without ever needing a bridge.

Unified Liquidity Pool


Most "multichain" DEXs fracture their liquidity across networks. With OctoMarket, the counterparty to every trade on every network is a unified liquidity pool of stablecoins. Regardless of which network a user deposits assets, they are seamlessly transfered to the basechain LP.

This faciliates seamless trades against the balance of the LP from any chain, with protocol liquidity & orders unified between them. This increases market depth, removes the need to use a bridge, and is a vastly better user experience.
